Hello and welcome to my little backwater website where I'll be featuring bits of the fiction, conlangs,
and other miscellaneous works I've been toying around with. There won't be much on here for a while,
as I'm busy working on a few (read, several) other projects that are time-sensitive, but at some point
I hope to begin putting stuff up regularly. I've yet to decide what fiction I'll put up, but I've thrown
a few things in the conlang section; I imagine the blog will remain empty longer than the others as I'm
not particularly inclined to writing posts, but perhaps I'll find some inspiration somewhere. In any case,
you can expect to find (at some future date) an archive of my writings on here. I do hope you enjoy them.
